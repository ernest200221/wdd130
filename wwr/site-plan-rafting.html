Overview

Purpose

By having an online presence, we make it easier for potential customers to find us. This includes local customers searching for rafting experiences, as well as tourists planning their trips online. Our website also allows us to interact with customers who leave their honest review of their experience online. This feedback helps us improve and provide the best services possible.

Audience

Our target audience for this website primarily includes adventure enthusiasts and thrill-seekers who are looking for exciting outdoor experiences. These individuals are typically between the ages of 18 to 45, active, and enjoy exploring nature through exciting activities like rafting. They are tech-savvy and rely heavily on online resources to plan their adventures.

Scenario questions:

  • Who are the target customers?
  • What do they want?
  • How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?
Answers:
  • Individuals typically between the ages of 18 to 45.
  • They want an exciting outdoor experience.
  • This site is accessible on any smartphone, laptop or computer with access to the internet.
